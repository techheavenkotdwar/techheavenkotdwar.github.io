Course Overview

The Diploma in Computer Applications (DCA) program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the various aspects of computer applications. The curriculum includes both theoretical and practical components, enabling students to develop the skills necessary for a successful career in IT.

Course Syllabus

3 months

Basic Computer Skills, HTML, Hindi/English Typing, Personality Development

6 months

C Language, CCC, Advanced Excel, Object-Oriented Programming (OOPs)

12 months

C++, Python, Web Designing/ Tally Prime with GST

Upon completing the DCA course, students will be equipped with the skills to pursue IT level roles such as:
